ariel lee

UX/UI Designer
LOS ANGELES, CA.

Since September 2016, I have been working Import and Export Operations at Freight Forwarding Company, Recommended Cargo. I work with clients on a daily basis to ensure their cargo is properly and safely delivered.
Throughout all this, my creative spirit has been left unfulfilled.

Designing has always played a huge role in my life, whether it's handcrafting customized birthday cards or creating t-shirt designs for my tennis team. Designs should not only be aesthetically pleasing, but also functional, fulfilling its intended purpose. Consequently, I strive for my designs to be simple yet intuitive, emphasizing aspects that make the product useful, while disregarding all that would demean it.

My goal as a designer is to create the most honest, user-friendly designs that showcase your product's value to users - the design essentially speaks for itself. I believe the best designs are those created with clients, through constant communication and revision. I will work with you to ensure you are happy with the end result.
